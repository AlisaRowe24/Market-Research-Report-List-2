<p><ul><li>Organic Medical Elastomers</li><li>Inorganic Medical Elastomers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical elastomers market is divided into two main types: organic and inorganic medical elastomers. Organic medical elastomers, derived from carbon-based materials, exhibit excellent flexibility, biocompatibility, and durability, making them suitable for applications such as tubing, seals, and medical devices. In contrast, inorganic medical elastomers are composed of non-carbon-based materials, such as siloxanes, which offer unique properties like high temperature resistance and chemical stability, making them ideal for specialized medical applications where extreme conditions are present.</p></p>
