<p><ul><li>Flanged</li><li>Threaded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam injector market is categorized into two main types: flanged and threaded. Flanged injectors feature a flat, lip-like edge that allows for easy attachment to piping systems, ensuring a secure and leak-proof connection. They are often used in industrial applications where robustness is essential. Threaded injectors, on the other hand, utilize a screw-like design for connection, allowing for easy installation and removal. This type is preferred in applications requiring flexibility and frequent maintenance. Both types serve crucial roles in steam generation processes.</p></p>
