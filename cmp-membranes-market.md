<p><p>CMP membranes are categorized into various market types based on their zone configurations, which denote the number of discrete areas designed for chemical mechanical polishing processes. The 5-zone, 6-zone, and 7-zone membranes feature increasingly complex designs, allowing for tailored polishing strategies and improved material removal rates. In contrast, below 5-zone configurations, such as 3-zone membranes, offer simpler solutions for applications where extensive zone flexibility is not required, balancing performance and cost-effectiveness for specific CMP needs.</p></p>
