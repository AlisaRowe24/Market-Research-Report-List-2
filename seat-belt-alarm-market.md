<p><p>The Seat Belt Alarm Market is categorized into two main types: Wireless Alarm Systems and Wired Alarm Systems. Wireless Alarm Systems utilize radiofrequency technology to notify passengers about unfastened seat belts without physical connections, enhancing installation flexibility and reducing wiring complexity. In contrast, Wired Alarm Systems are directly connected to the vehicle's circuitry, providing a more traditional and reliable approach. Both systems aim to improve safety by ensuring compliance with seat belt regulations and increasing awareness among passengers.</p></p>
