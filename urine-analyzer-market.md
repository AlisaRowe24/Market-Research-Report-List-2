<p><p>The urine analyzer market is primarily segmented into two main types: microscopy and chemistry methods. Microscopy methods involve examining urine samples under a microscope to identify cellular elements, crystals, and bacteria, providing detailed information about various conditions. In contrast, chemistry methods focus on analyzing the chemical composition of urine using test strips or automated systems, detecting parameters such as pH, protein, glucose, and electrolytes. Both methods complement each other, offering a comprehensive approach to urinary tract health assessment and diagnostics.</p></p>
