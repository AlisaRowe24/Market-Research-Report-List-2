<p><p>FPC antennas, or Flexible Printed Circuit antennas, are categorized into two main markets: internal and external antennas. Internal antennas are designed for compact devices like smartphones, tablets, and wearables, offering space-saving solutions and optimal performance within limited environments. In contrast, external antennas are utilized in applications requiring extended range and enhanced signal quality, often found in vehicles, routers, and industrial equipment. Both market types leverage the advantages of FPC technology, including lightweight, flexibility, and ease of integration.</p></p>
