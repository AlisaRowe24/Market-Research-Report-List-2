<p><p>The Handheld 3D Scanner market is primarily categorized into two types: Structured Light Scanners and Laser Scanners. Structured Light Scanners utilize projected light patterns to capture 3D data, making them suitable for detailed surface analysis and rapid scanning of small objects. In contrast, Laser Scanners emit laser beams to measure distances and create precise 3D models, excelling in larger-scale projects and environments. Both technologies are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, heritage preservation, and design due to their accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
