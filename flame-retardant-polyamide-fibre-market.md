<p><ul><li>Copolymerization</li><li>Blending Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flame retardant polyamide fibre market can be categorized into two main types based on production methods: copolymerization and blending. Copolymerization involves the synthesis of polyamide fibres that integrate flame-retardant chemical elements directly into their molecular structure, enhancing fire resistance. In contrast, the blending method combines conventional polyamide fibres with flame-retardant additives, resulting in materials that exhibit improved safety characteristics. Both approaches aim to provide textiles and industrial applications with enhanced fire safety while maintaining desired mechanical properties.</p></p>
