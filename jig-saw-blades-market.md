<p><p>The jigsaw blades market primarily consists of two types: T-Shank and U-Shank blades. T-Shank blades feature a tang shaped like a "T," providing better grip and stability in most jigsaw models, making them popular among professionals and DIY enthusiasts. U-Shank blades, with a U-shaped base, are typically used in older jigsaws and are less common today. The choice between these types affects cutting precision, compatibility, and user experience, shaping consumer preferences in the jigsaw blades market.</p></p>
