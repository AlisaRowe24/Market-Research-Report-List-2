<p><ul><li>Normal EVA</li><li>Anti-PID EVA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EVA film markets are categorized into Normal EVA and Anti-PID EVA based on their properties and applications. Normal EVA film is widely used in laminating photovoltaic cells and offers basic protective features. In contrast, Anti-PID EVA film is specifically engineered to reduce potential-induced degradation, enhancing solar panel longevity and efficiency. This makes Anti-PID EVA increasingly popular in high-efficiency solar applications where durability against moisture and electrical stress is crucial, catering to the growing demand for reliable renewable energy solutions.</p></p>
