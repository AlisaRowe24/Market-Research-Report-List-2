<p><p>Field-mount temperature transmitters encompass various technologies including thermistors, bimetallic temperature transmitters, resistive temperature detectors (RTDs), fiber optic temperature transmitters, and thermocouples. Thermistors offer high sensitivity and rapid response, while bimetallic transmitters provide straightforward mechanical temperature measurement. RTDs ensure precise readings over a wide range, whereas fiber optic transmitters excel in harsh environments and electromagnetic interference immunity. Thermocouples are favored for their versatility and broad temperature range, making these devices essential across industries for accurate temperature monitoring and control.</p></p>
