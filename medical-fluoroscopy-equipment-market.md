<p><ul><li>Fixed Fluoroscopy Equipment</li><li>C-Arms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical fluoroscopy equipment market primarily comprises two types: fixed fluoroscopy equipment and C-arms. Fixed fluoroscopy equipment is stationary and commonly found in hospitals, providing high-resolution imaging for various procedures. This type is typically used for advanced diagnostic and therapeutic applications. C-arms, on the other hand, are portable fluoroscopic devices that offer flexibility and mobility, making them suitable for surgical environments. They enable real-time imaging, enhancing precision during minimally invasive procedures, thereby expanding their utility in various medical fields.</p></p>
