<p><p>The polyethylene (PE) foam market is categorized primarily into Non-Cross-Linked Polyethylene (Non-XLPE) foam and Cross-Linked Polyethylene (XLPE) foam. Non-XLPE foam is characterized by its softer texture and is widely used for cushioning and insulation in packaging and construction applications. In contrast, XLPE foam is denser and features superior resistance to moisture and chemicals, making it ideal for automotive, industrial, and high-performance insulation applications. Both types are valued for their lightweight nature and versatility across various industries.</p></p>
