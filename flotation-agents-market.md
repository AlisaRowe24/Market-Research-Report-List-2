<p><ul><li>Collector</li><li>Foaming Agent</li><li>Regulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flotation agents in the market are crucial for enhancing mineral separation processes in mining and mineral processing. Collectors are chemical additives that increase the hydrophobicity of target minerals, promoting their attachment to air bubbles. Foaming agents stabilize the froth in flotation processes, ensuring effective separation of minerals. Regulators, including pH modifiers and depressants, control the conditions within the flotation cell to optimize performance and prevent unwanted mineral flotation. Together, these agents improve the efficiency and effectiveness of mineral recovery.</p></p>
