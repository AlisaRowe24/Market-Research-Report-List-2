<p><strong>The Screw Doser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric Screw Doser</li><li>Gravimetric Screw Doser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screw doser market is segmented into volumetric and gravimetric types. Volumetric screw dosers measure materials based on volume using a rotating screw, ensuring consistent flow rates for bulk materials. In contrast, gravimetric screw dosers measure the weight of the materials being dispensed, providing higher accuracy and adaptability to variations in material density. Both types are essential in various industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and plastics, where precise dosing is crucial for quality control and operational efficiency.</p></p>
