<p><strong>The Medical Hand Orthosis (HO) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic Orthotics</li><li>Static Orthotics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Hand Orthosis (HO) market is divided into two primary types: dynamic orthotics and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to provide movement while supporting the hand, often facilitating rehabilitation by allowing controlled motion during recovery. In contrast, static orthotics immobilize the hand to stabilize injuries or conditions, offering protection and support without movement. Both types play crucial roles in therapy, enhancing functional recovery and improving quality of life for individuals with hand impairments.</p></p>
