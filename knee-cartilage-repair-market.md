<p><p>The knee cartilage repair market encompasses various techniques aimed at restoring cartilage function. Arthroscopic chondroplasty involves smoothing damaged cartilage via minimally invasive surgery. Autologous chondrocyte implantation uses a patient's cells to regenerate tissue. Osteochondral graft transplantation involves transferring healthy cartilage and bone. Cell-based cartilage resurfacing utilizes stem cells to promote healing. Implants transplant involves the use of synthetic materials for support. Microfracture creates small holes in the bone to encourage cartilage growth, while the "other products" category includes emerging therapies and devices.</p></p>
