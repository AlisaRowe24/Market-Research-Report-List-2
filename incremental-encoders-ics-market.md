<p><strong>The Incremental Encoders ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Encoders</li><li>Transmissive Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Incremental encoder ICs are essential in various applications for position and speed sensing. The market primarily consists of reflective encoders and transmissive encoders. Reflective encoders utilize light sensors to detect reflected signals from a rotating target, making them suitable for surfaces that can bounce light. Conversely, transmissive encoders operate by having a light source pass through an opaque disc with slots, allowing the sensor to detect interruptions. Each type provides different advantages in terms of accuracy, environmental resilience, and application suitability.</p></p>
