<p><p>The Inline Automated X-ray Inspection (AXI) market is divided into two main types: 2D AXI and 3D AXI. 2D AXI focuses on capturing two-dimensional images of electronic assemblies to detect defects, providing quick and effective inspections. In contrast, 3D AXI employs advanced imaging technology to create three-dimensional representations of components, enabling deeper analysis of solder joints and internal structures. Both types enhance quality control in manufacturing, ensuring higher reliability and performance in electronic devices.</p></p>
