<p><strong>The 300mm Wafer Used Electrostatic Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 300mm wafer used electrostatic chuck market comprises two primary types: Coulomb type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type. Coulomb-type chucks use electric fields to hold wafers by generating forces between the chuck and the wafer surface. In contrast, JR-type chucks utilize a combination of electric fields and dielectric materials to enhance adhesion, providing better stability and uniformity for wafer processing. Both types are crucial for advanced semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring precise handling and improved yield during production.</p></p>
